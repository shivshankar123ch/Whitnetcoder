What is in-company training?


In-company training courses bring together trainees with the same expectations and a similar level. Each employee can thus benefit from the training easily and put it into practice without delay. In-company training almost always involves an external training organisation

The objectives of the training differ according to the needs of the learners, the company and the training organisation chosen. In general, the modalities can be tailored to meet the needs and training objectives of the company with precision.

Why do an in-company training?

You have understood: the overall objective of in-company training is to train trainees from the same company so that they are operational in their missions. This type of training has many advantages. We present them to you.


1. Bringing together and training employees around a common project

2. Valuing the skills of its employees

3. Flexible training methods
